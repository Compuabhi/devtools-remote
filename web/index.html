DevTools Remote enables you to share access to a browser tab,
so it can be debugged remotely via Chrome DevTools.

1. Install Chrome extension

Share access to access any tab, anytime, by pressing the Share button in Chrome’s toolbar.

2. Click Share button

Easily and safely get a link to your new debug session, just by clicking the Share button.

3. Share unique link

To get access to your chrome tab, all the other person has to do is to click the link you send them.

That's it!

DevTools Remote is an exploration of how cross-browser
remote debugging protocols can be used to bring value to real people.
